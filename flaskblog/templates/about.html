Admin

The role of the Admin is very simple, Admins only can register people into the Web Application, also, Admins can do the same things as simple Users do for example: They can create expenses. Another feature of the Admin role is that they can edit an expense that has been made from another user but the can only edit only the "Client Project" and "Saggezza or Client" fields.

Manager

The Manager role is simplier than the Admins role. How is that ? Whenever an expense is made the current status of that expense is "Pending" so the manager has to open the expense and review it, if it's okay for him/her they can click on the verify button to verify the current expense that they are viewing.

User

Now the User role is limited to only create expenses unlike the other two roles. Also do not try to access pages that no Users are allowed you will get error "403" Access Forbidden.

How the web Application works

To use the web application you have to login first if you dont have an account contact to your company to make you one. Once you logged in you will see at the top right that you have "Create Expense", "Account" and "Logout" if your role is Manager or User, if your role is Admin then you will have one more that says "Register" because only the ones with the "Admin" role can register other users. You can go to the "Account" page to see your personal information such as your profile picture, your email address and the username, also, you can change your username, email address and profile picture whenever you want to. Next we have the "Create expense" page where you can create an expense report when you are there to create an expense report you have to fill all the fields, you can leave behind only the "Cost in EUR" and the "Cost in USD" those fields are not required so you can leave the behind. Once the expense report is created it will be showed up on the "Home" page where everybody can see it. When you navigate your self to the "Home" page you will see all the expenses there and the current status of the expense report. If the current status is "Pending" then that means that is not being reviewed yet by a Manager. Keep a close eye on your expense reports because they might be some changes on them by the Admin. If you forget your password you can press on the forgot password email and you will be navigated on another page to put your email address there, once you put your email address there and click on the submit button you will receive an email on the email address that you put before. When you you open that email a link will appear when you click on that address you will be navigated to a page where you can choose a different password, click on the submit button and you are all done
